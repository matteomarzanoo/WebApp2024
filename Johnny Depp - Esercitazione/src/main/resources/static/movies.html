Top 3 Movies of Johnny Depp
Pirates of the Caribbean: The Curse of the Black Pearl

In this swashbuckling adventure, Captain Jack Sparrow teams up with a young blacksmith to rescue the governor's daughter from cursed pirates.

Edward Scissorhands

This modern fairy tale tells the story of an artificial man named Edward, who has scissors for hands and struggles to find his place in a suburban community.

Alice in Wonderland

In Tim Burton's whimsical adaptation, Alice returns to the fantastical world she first encountered as a child, where she reunites with the Mad Hatter and battles the Red Queen.
